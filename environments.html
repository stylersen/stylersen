ONE DAY MEGASTRUCTURE
modeling/texturing/rendering
This project was designed to be a departure from my usual schoolwork, and more in line with what personally interests me. Technology becoming something bigger than us, something we can't control is an idea that I find myself constantly thinking about. I decided to see what I could make in the span of one day. This is the end result. Nothing out of this world, but a decent result for just one day. This was my first time using displacement maps and volumes/VDBs in Blender, and both were challenging but rewarding to learn. I tried to get a sense of scale in this render, but a better end product would've been an image that inluded an asset for scale. Some trees throughout the mountains would've really made this one complete, in my opinion.
