About Ty

I graduated from Boise State University in May 2026 with a bachelors degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile. Throughout my time as a student, I learned a great deal about technology and how we interact with it. My work now tends to revolve around these themes: technology, how it affects us, and our future with it. As such, hard surface models and environments are what I excel at. I mainly work in the 3rd dimension, although it eventually ends up in the 2nd. I am proficient with Blender, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, , and Unity. I plan on becoming skilled with Substance Painter, Davinci Resolve, Zbrush, Plasticity.
